What makes Private International English School stand out

Private International English School (PIES) embarked on its journey in September 2010 providing a comprehensive CBSE curriculum for students from Kindergarten till Grade 12. Today, with almost 2,000 students, PIES stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the region. The school proudly holds its affiliation from the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) in New Delhi and boasts full accreditation from the Department of Education and Knowledge (ADEK) and received ‘Very Good’ rating in its inspection in 2018 and maintained in for the last 4 consecutive years. PIES also takes pride in its expansive campus, making it one of the largest educational institutions in the area and has turned out to be a hub of learning looked up to.
